# Content sharing & marketplace

**Status:** requested (2026-06-13).

We want it easy for other devs to create their own content (modules, scenes, etc.) and share/use it, and for us to be able to import their stuff easily.

## Ask

- Make it **easy for other devs to author** their own content (modules, scenes, …).
- Make it **easy to share** that content and to **import / depend on** other people's content.
- Be able to **remix / modify** imported content if we want to.

## Eventual goal

- An embedded **"asset store / marketplace"** where folks can:
  - **search** and find things they like,
  - things are **rated**,
  - things are **moderated**.

## Scope to start

- **No money** — purely about making it easy to **share and depend on** other people's things, and then **remix/modify**.
